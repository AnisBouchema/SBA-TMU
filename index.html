What is the SBA

A different kind of business club

The SBA sits at the intersection of business, sustainability, and real life. We challenge the idea that profit and purpose are opposites — and we prove it through education, events, and community.

Resource Wisdom

Our guiding philosophy that circular thinking, efficiency, and long-term impact are the smartest competitive advantages of our generation.

ESG & Sustainable Finance

Learn how environmental, social, and governance factors drive real financial returns and how capital is shifting toward ethical long-term projects.

Ethical Leadership

Developing decision-makers who think beyond next quarter and account for future generations as part of every business strategy.

Real Community

Open to every TMU student regardless of program. Business, engineering, design, arts — if you're curious, you belong here.

The Bigger Idea

Sustainable business as a lifestyle

The same principles that make a business last — balance, efficiency, thinking long-term, not burning out your resources — apply directly to how you live. We don't choose between ambition and wellbeing. We build both.

01

Stay Ambitious

Hustle with purpose. Set big goals, work hard, and pursue your career with real intention. Ambition is celebrated here — and backed by a community that holds you to it.

02

Rest & Recharge

Breaks aren't weakness — they're strategy. You can't build anything meaningful running on empty. Recovery is part of the plan, not a detour from it.

03

Protect Your Health

Mental, physical, social. Sustainable living means taking care of yourself today so you can keep going tomorrow — and for the decades after that.
